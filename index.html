Pre-Session

DURATION: 1H | ONLINE
  • Share your current season: change, crisis, liminal phase, or desire (wealth, love, creativity, clarity)
  • Bring your birth data; your astrology chart is used to tune the guidance
  • Clarify your core intention through guided questions

Gathering

DURATION: 20MIN | ON YOUR OWN ACTIVITY
  • Go into nature with your intention in mind
  • Forage a few items that match what you want to call in or release (leaves, branches, stones, flowers, etc.)
  • Receive a simple email handout with examples and guidelines

Golden Bough Workshop

DURATION: 2H | IN PERSON
  • Bring your collected items and a chosen container (jar, vase, bowl, ceramic)
  • Learn how to build your altar and create offerings with flowers and natural elements
  • Leave with a living altar and a repeatable ritual structure you can continue at home
